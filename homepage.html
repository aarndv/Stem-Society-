About Us

The FEU HS STEM Society (STEM Soc) is a strand-based academic organization in FEU High School. This firmly enhances the students' skills, excellence, competence, and character. It also serves as a training ground for learners who aspire to be innovators, scientists, engineers, architects, and other specializations who can lead and be good contributors in the field of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

This organization aims to bring every STEM student to a bright FEUture.

Mission

The organization shall provide and enhance skills, knowledge, competence, and values to its members.

Vision

The organization shall produce and develop innovators, scientists, engineers, and technologists capable of leading and making significant contributions in the field of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

Goals

Provide academic aids and support to its members.

Inspire and support its members in designing innovative research projects.

Collaborate and build partnership with professionals and the industry.

Offer a supportive environment that allows its participants to openly discuss ideas and cultivate and utilize their skills.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a member of FEUHS STEM Society?

Everyone in the STEM strand is considered a member of the organization. However, students who wish to be a part of the working committees may apply but are not obliged to do so.

Is STEM Society strictly for STEM students only?

Yes. If you are from another strand, you are already a member of your respective strand society whether ABM Society, HUMMS Society or GAS Society.

Can I apply in the organization even after the Organization Hunt?

Yes. The FEUHS STEM Society is open for applications throughout the year. Just send a message to: feuhsstemsoc@gmail.com or to Ysabel Dagohoy (President) or to Carmela Mariz Tenorio (Vice-President).
